Specification
  • Blade Span: Usually ranging from 42 inches to 60 inches, determining the diameter of the fan blades.
  • Number of Blades: Typically equipped with 3 to 5 blades, affecting airflow and aesthetic appeal.
  • Motor Power: Measured in watts or horsepower, indicating the fan's ability to rotate the blades efficiently.
  • Speed Settings: Offering multiple speed options for adjusting airflow intensity, often including settings like low, medium, and high.
  • Material: Constructed from durable materials like metal, wood, or plastic, ensuring longevity and performance.
  • Finish: Available in various finishes such as brushed nickel, matte black, bronze, or white, complementing different interior styles.
